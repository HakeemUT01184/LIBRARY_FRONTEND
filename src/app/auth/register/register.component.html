Library Information

  • Once you create an account, an approval request will be sent to the admin. If approved, your account will be created.
  • You can have a maximum of 3 books ordered that have not been returned.
  • You can keep a book for a maximum of 10 days.
  • If a book is not returned after 10 days, you will receive an email reminder.
  • A fine of 50Rs will be imposed every day for every overdue book, and you will be notified via email.
  • If your fine exceeds 500Rs or the book is damaged, the admin may block your account.
